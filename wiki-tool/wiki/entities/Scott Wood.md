> "Establishing Standards of Best Practice in Automated Scoring," authored by Scott Wood, Erin Yao, Lisa Haisfield, and Susan Lottridge and published as an ACT Research Technical Brief in July 2021. [[Wood Yao Haisfield Lottridge 2021 - Standards of Best Practice in Automated Scoring]]
