> [!tldr]
> ACT, Inc. is a US educational-assessment organization that publishes the ACT college-readiness exam and, in 2021, a technical brief codifying 10 standards of best practice for automated scoring.

## Overview

> [!source] Publisher of the 10-standards codification.
> The ACT Research Technical Brief "Establishing Standards of Best Practice in Automated Scoring" (Wood, Yao, Haisfield, & Lottridge, July 2021) is published by ACT, Inc. and authored by ACT AS research staff. [[Wood Yao Haisfield Lottridge 2021 - Standards of Best Practice in Automated Scoring]]

> [!source] ACT intends to review the AS standards annually.
> "AS staff at ACT plan to review these standards on an annual basis. This ensures that the standards are based on current best practices and new research findings. Major updates to these standards will be published as needed." [[Wood Yao Haisfield Lottridge 2021 - Standards of Best Practice in Automated Scoring]]

> [!source] ACT invites external feedback on the standards.
> Wood et al. solicit feedback at `CRASE@act.org` with the aim of contributing to industry-wide standards. [[Wood Yao Haisfield Lottridge 2021 - Standards of Best Practice in Automated Scoring]]

## Relationships

- Publishes work by [[Scott Wood]], [[Erin Yao]], [[Lisa Haisfield]], [[Susan Lottridge]].
- Peer testing organization to [[Educational Testing Service]] (ETS); both develop automated-scoring systems and are cited across the standards literature.
